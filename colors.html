Colors

Effective UX design begins with the simplicity of type.

Example

#006FAE
#05AA0D
#F00000
#E3E3E3

Commentary

The above palette captures the primary accent colors that are used across Commerce. They serve as a guideline for color selection. Use these accents sparingly. Even though these colors are called out, they should not be used without good reason. Do not use these colors as a way to make things "stand out" or to increase the level of "intuitiveness."
